About Me

My name is Mutaz Hennawi and I am a creative web developer. I'm currently based in Edmonton, AB. My main focus lies on full-stack, JavaScript and ReactJS development. I also have rich experience as a backend developer, mostly in MERN (MongoDB, ExpressJS, React, Node.js) stack. My first contact with web development was more than 6 years ago in high school. I liked how I can create something from nothing, so I began programming with PHP. I learned everything by myself from the available resources & work experience and I gradually became a problem-solver and a full-stack developer!
